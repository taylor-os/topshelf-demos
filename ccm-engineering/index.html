Our Services

Engineering judgment when the stakes are highest.

Whether you're buying a home, questioning a repair quote, or need documentation for a lender, our licensed engineers give you a clear, defensible answer you can act on.

Pre-Purchase Inspections

Buying a home? We evaluate the foundation and structure before you close — so you know exactly what you're buying and can negotiate with confidence.

Structural Engineering Reports

Detailed, stamped assessments of foundations, framing and load-bearing elements — the documentation lenders, insurers and buyers rely on.

Foundation-Repair Evaluations

Told you need thousands in foundation work? We assess whether the repair is actually necessary and whether the proposed scope is right.

Second Opinions

An independent, unbiased engineering review of another company's findings — no repair to sell, just an honest professional assessment.

Engineer Letters & Certifications

Signed and sealed letters and certifications for lenders, insurers, permits and closings — turned around quickly when timelines are tight.

Crack & Movement Analysis

Cracks, sticking doors, uneven floors? We diagnose the cause, tell you if it's structural, and lay out what — if anything — to do about it.
