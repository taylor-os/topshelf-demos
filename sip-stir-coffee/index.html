Our Story

A cozy corner of Highland Village.

Sip Stir isn't a franchise — it's a neighborhood coffee house where you'll often find the owners behind the counter, pulling shots and learning your name. Settle in, plug in, and stay a while.

  • Owner-operated — friendly faces who genuinely care about your cup.
  • Built for laptops — plenty of outlets, free WiFi and a warm, quiet vibe.
  • Outdoor seating — a sunny patio for coffee, catch-ups and slow mornings.
  • Made your way — dine-in, curbside pickup, delivery and gift cards.
